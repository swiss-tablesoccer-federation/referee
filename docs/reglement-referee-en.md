## Introduction

The Refereeing Regulations govern all matters relating to
refereeing within the STF.

## Scope

This document governs refereeing within the Swiss Tablesoccer Federation.
Individual rules are defined in the relevant ITSF and STF documents and
are not part of these regulations.

## Refereeing

According to the ITSF definition, the STF is responsible for training referees up to and including the
national level.

## Integration

In principle, referees must adhere to the Referee Code defined by the ITSF.
However, the rules/definitions contained therein may be overridden by this document.
In case of doubt, the rules in this document apply.
